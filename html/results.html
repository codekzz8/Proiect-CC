What does the score of the text mean?
The score ranges from -1.0 (negative) and 1.0 (positive) and corresponds to the overall emotional leaning of the text.

What does the magnitude of the text mean?
The magnitude indicates the overall strength of emotion (both positive and negative) within the given text, between 0.0 and +inf. Unlike score, magnitude is not normalized; each expression of emotion within the text (both positive and negative) contributes to the text's magnitude (so longer text blocks may have greater magnitudes.
